Semantic tags

Sementic tags are used to optimize seo and also tells the google which part of website contains what content

example - header, nav, aside, main, article, figure, footer


header - it contains heading parts of website like nav bar
nav - indicates nav bar
aside - used for sidebars or content i.e. tangentially related to main content
footer - copyright info, location, important links
figure or figcaption - used for charts, images, diagrams
main - main content
time - date and time
